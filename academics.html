CCE METHOD OF EDUCATION from First to Eighth std. The Project and Assesment with Scholastic & Co-Scholastic Evaluation empowers exclusive, enriched learning experiences to students on subject Learning & activities.

SMART CLASS,atmosphere are well-determined for the students of K.G to tenth STD on Visual Learning Concepts.

Smart Class is a solution designed to help teachers in meeting with the new challenges and developing students’ abilities and performance. It helps the teachers to access multimedia content and information that can be used for teaching students more effectively. It helps the teachers in expressing their views and ensures that every student understands and learns the concepts. A well designed module allows a student to visualise the concept much better than static images.

ASPIRE - LEARNING, for seventh and eighth STD & nineth STD inspires Communicative skills & Personality developement.

XSEED is a proven and research based academic program for schools that builds thinking skills & problem solving confidence in children. XSEED children ask more questions, can write in their own words, like doing word problems in mathematics, can complete their homework on their own, are not afraid to speak-up in English, persist longer in solving problems, and score well on tests.

The School inculcates team spirit and healthy competitive spirit among the students by following the House System. The four houses are named after the four great leaders –Felix,Britto,Bosco,Xavier – in order to inspire the young minds to follow the footsteps of these exemplary personalities.
