Founder's message:

Every Child who enters the school is a gift. First it is God’s gift to its parents and family. But also it is a gift to the schools, to the country and to the world at large.

IF THEY ARE TRAINED AT SCHOOL, THEY CAN BECOME USEFUL TO THE SOCIETY OTHERWISE HEADACHE TO THE SOCIETY.

The Creator has a unique destiny for each individual. What a marvelous thing it is to be co-worker in the plan of the Creator. Parents and Teachers are given the glorious opportunity to help mould these children to their fullest capacity. They can help them, to be aglow with the spirit and love of the creator and bring them to that self knowledge and awareness of his presence in the human person.

In such an atmosphere, can there be any sort of disrespect for human life if young minds and hearts are trained to respect self and others?

The joy of the EDUCATOR can be compared to that of the potter? The POTTER gives shape to the clay. The finished product is his joy. So too does the teacher form and mould the child. The result is not merely a stative product, but a mature, well developed genuine human person. In this way the educator can be said to recreator. He / she re-creates the child to develop it to its full potential.

We encourage our teachers. May they spare the efforts to kindle the flame of light in every child entrusted to their care.

The alphabets in the name itself tells clearly his noble qualities. Our late principal MR. C.FELIX cared the STUDENTS not only in their studies but also in all the activities. He guided the students

To Get ready for School,
To plan out the Works systematically,
To obey and follow traffic rules on road,
To behave in the class room,
To behave well wherever they go,
To do the works by themselves,
To be neat and tidy,
To take care of the health during all seasons,
To respect elders and others…….
