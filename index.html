Welcome to St. John’s Matriculation School

St.John’s Matriculation School is run by St.john’s Educational Trust. The School was founded in June 1979. It is a co-educational English Medium School recognized by the Govt. of TAMIL NADU. Ever since its foundation pupil of all caste and creed are admitted here.

St. John’s School education is being inspired by the life and teaching of Jesus Christ. It is based on the principles of character formation which gives St.John’s Education its special character.

In general St.John’s School aims, at the integral personal and total formation of the students. To this special efforts are made.

Our Motto is LEARN TO LEAD
